I am currently an AI Resident at Google Brain. I completed my Ph.D. (thesis) at Rice University in 2018 under the supervision of Richard Baraniuk. I received my M.Sc. (thesis) at Rice University in 2014, and my B.Sc. degree from the Sharif University of Technology in 2011. My research focus during the PhD period was on the application of Machine Learning in solving inverse problems. Right now, I am focusing on Reinforcement Learning and it's connection to Causal Inference.
